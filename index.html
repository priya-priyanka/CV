Lead Recruitement Consultant

A Recruiting Lead is a position within a company that is responsible for attracting and sourcing new talent for the organization. This role is often found within the Human Resources department and reports to the Head of Talent Acquisition or a similar senior-level manager

  • You probably know that a Recruiter can help you find a job
  • Determining current staffing needs and producing forecasts. Developing talent acquisition strategies and hiring plans. Leading employment branding initiatives
  • Lead Recruiters are typically experienced and will be entrusted with more complex or higher-level recruitment projects
